Ideal Job

This job is a position advertised by an IT recruitment agency for a network engineer with level 3 experience. It involves providing a technical leadership and support role for information technology networks and systems including design, research and analysis of business networks with a strong emphasis on inter personal skills. It is appealing to me as it involves a lot of problem solving and interaction with clients.

Skills Required

Applicant must be certified level 3 IT technician with strong problem solving skills and good interpersonal and management capabilities. The ability to work under pressure would also be in Information Technology degree is required.

Skills I Have And How To Get There

I currently have fairly good problem solving skills with the work I currently do, I also have fairly good interpersonal skills as I have to liaise with clients and other trades through my current job. To get the experience and accreditations to be able to apply for a job like this I would have to finish my IT degree, get an entry level job as a network engineer and develop my skills and experience through workin o the job.
