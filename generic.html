Interest In IT

I developed an interest in IT through my job working as Audio Visual/Systems integrator installing/programming; audio visual systems, automated lighting systems, home networks and control systems. I particularly enjoy problem solving and making things work. I find it both extremely challenging and rewarding at the same time. The experience I have is mostly network related including setting up robust smaller scale networks for the use of integration, content streaming. This involves wiring and commissioning routers, switches, wireless access points as well as end user devices and IP products.
